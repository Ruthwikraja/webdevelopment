About Movie

The blood-soaked land of Kolar Gold Fields (KGF) Has a new overlord now. Rocky, whose name strikes fear in

order; enemies are clamoring for revenge and conspiring for his downfall. Bloodier battles and darker days await
as Rocky continues on his quest for unchallenged supremacy.
